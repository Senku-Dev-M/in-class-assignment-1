01. What is it?

Frustration is a common emotional response to opposition. It arises when the pursuit of a goal is blocked or hindered.

Expectation Reality Frustration

02. Common Scenarios

Technical Failure

When technology fails at critical moments.
Ex: Blue screen before saving work.

Ambiguity

Unclear or constantly changing goals.
Ex: "What exactly do I have to do?"

Team Friction

Lack of communication and blockers.
Ex: Waiting indefinitely for a reply.

03. How to avoid it?

Communicate

Ask clear questions. Don't assume anything. Define dates and expectations from the start.

Organize

Break big tasks into small steps. Use checklists to reduce mental load.

Adapt

Accept what you cannot control. Focus on your reaction, not the problem itself.
